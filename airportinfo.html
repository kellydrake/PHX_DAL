Departing from: Phoenix

Parking/Transportation

  • Terminal 4 garage has 4 parking zones: A, B, C, & D. We recommend parking in the zone which corresponds to your gate number.
  • See the map for parking zone locations. Remember to take pictures of the pillar closest to your vehicle so you can locate it when you return.
  • Rates: $4 per hour. $27 per day.
  • Check out Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport's website for all the details.
  • East Economy Lot: Ride the free PHX Sky Train to terminal 4. The train arrives every 3-5 minutes and operates 24/7.
  • Rates: $4/hour. $12/daily for the uncovered lot and $14/daily for the garage.
  • If you're getting dropped off, the terminal 4 passenger drop off areas are on level 2, north and south curbs.
  • Other ways of getting to the airport include: Rideshare, The Parking Spot, taxis, prearranged cars/shuttles, public transportation, and hotel shuttles.

Ticket Counter/Baggage

  • Our ticket counter is located on level 2. We recommend arriving at least 150 minutes prior to your scheduled departure.
  • Self-service kiosks: Please use the kiosks located in front of our ticket counter to retrieve your boarding pass and to tag your checked luggage.
  • Ensure you check your luggage at least 45 minutes prior to your scheduled departure.
  • For more details, visit our help center to learn more about the self-service kiosks.
  • After tagging your bags at the kiosk, drop off your luggage with one of our Customer Service Agents at the ticket counter.
  • Head up the escalators towards the TSA checkpoint associated with your gate number.
  • For wheelchair assistance: At terminal 4, use door 26 from the South curb to request assistance from a Southwest Employee if possible. You can also request a wheelchair once inside at this same location. For more details about requesting assistance in the airport, please visit the Customers with Disabilities page.
  • Southwest gates are: C: 1-4, 6-9, 11-14, 16-19, and D: 1-8.

TSA Checkpoints/Wait Times

  • In terminal 4, there are 4 security checkpoints associated with each gate.
  • To help ensure a smoother TSA screening process, check out TSA's website before your trip.
  • The TSA also has some awesome travel tips on their website if you haven't traveled in a while.
  • Check out the security wait times shown on this page as well as the TSA PreCheck hours before you go.

Food Options

Arriving in: Dallas (Love Field)

Baggage Claim

  • The baggage claim is located on the first floor.
  • After leaving the gate area, look for signs towards ground transportation.
  • Once past the Love Landing, walk down the stairs/escalator, and turn right down the hallway.
  • There are 4 baggage carousels and display screens to indicate which carousel your luggage will come out of.
  • Rideshare pickup: For app-based rideshare services, they can pick you up outside on the lower level roadway.
  • Rental cars: The rental car facilities can be found in the hallway leading up to the baggage claim area.
  • For more details, please be sure to visit Dallas Love Field Airport's website.

Transportation/Parking

  • Parking garages A, B and C are located adjacent to the Love Field terminal.
  • The Skybridge connecting Garages A and B to the terminal is on level three of the parking garages. The Garage C Skybridge bypasses the ticketing wing for passengers not requiring a printed boarded pass or checking luggage.
  • Customers may pay with their NTTA TollTag, with cash or credit upon exiting the garage or at a Skidata kiosk located near the garage entrances. Parking is limited to 60 days.
  • Rates:
    • Garage A: $15/day
    • Garage B: Covered - $9/day. Roof uncovered - $7/day.
    • Garage C: Covered - $15/day. Roof uncovered - $13/day. Second level Dallas Mavericks - $15/day. Third-level premium - $25/day.
